Sherk Pong Docs

Hey there, welcome to Sherk Pong!
This is a chill Pong-style game where each match goes up to 5 points. Whoever hits 5 first wins that match.

If you manage to win 3 matches, you become the ultimate champion!

How to move:
- On a computer, drag your mouse up or down to control Sherk’s paddle.
- On mobile, just drag your finger on the screen. Easy enough, right?

Pepe’s AI:
- Pepe (or Bonk) follows the ball with a slight delay. He won't miss often, so be ready!

Pro tips:
- Watch out if the score hits 4–4—things get crazy with “Deuce!” and possible “Match Point!” messages if someone might clinch the series.
- The ball gets faster with every bounce, so keep your reflexes sharp!
