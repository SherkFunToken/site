Sherk Pong Docs

Hey there, welcome to Sherk Pong!

- This is a best-of-3 Pong game where each match goes up to 5 points.
- If you get 3 matches first, you're the champion.

Movement:
- On PC, move your mouse up/down to control Sherk.
- On mobile, just drag your finger on the screen to move the paddle.

Pepe's AI:
- Pepe (Bonk) tries to follow the ball. He won't miss often, so watch out!

Pro Tips:
- The ball speeds up each time it hits a paddle, so keep calm and be ready for faster bounces.
- If the score reaches 4–4, you'll see “Deuce!” and possibly “Match Point!” messages if somebody can clinch the overall best-of-3.
- Have fun, and may the best Pong hero win!
